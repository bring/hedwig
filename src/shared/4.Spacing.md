
Our design systems is based on a few number of spacings.

> 24px is our default spacing

</div>

--hw-spacing-smallest: 6px;
--hw-spacing-smaller: 12px;
--hw-spacing-small: 18px;
--hw-spacing: 24px;
--hw-spacing-large: 36px;
--hw-spacing-larger: 48px;
